Get to know me!

I was born in Congo-Brazzaville to a Congolese mother and a Nigerian father, which gave me the gift of bilingualism—I'm fluent in both English and French, although I primarily write in English. From a young age, I’ve been drawn to puzzles and board games like chess, Monopoly, and Scrabble, excelling at them and nurturing my problem-solving skills. My favorite subject in primary school was mathematics, and this passion continued into secondary school, where I joined the science department and discovered programming. My first programming language was C++, which I taught myself using SoloLearn. Over time, I expanded my interests to cryptography, blockchain, and forex trading. When I began my university studies for a BTech in Mathematics, I decided to pursue a career in Data engineering. Drawing from my passion for creating algorithms and unraveling challenges, I became fascinated with building processes to extract, transform, and load data across systems efficiently. Designing automated workflows and scalable solutions deepened my love for data engineering, as it allows me to apply my analytical skills to real-world problems. Today, I combine my strong foundation in mathematics, logical thinking, and programming expertise to tackle complex data challenges and create innovative solutions that drive insights and impact.

I also like sharing content related to the stuff that I have learned over the years in GitHub so it can help other people of the GitHub Community. Feel free to Connect or Follow me on my Linkedin and Github where I post useful content related to Data Engineering and Programming

I'm open to Job opportunities where I can contribute, learn and grow. If you have a good opportunity that matches my skills and experience then don't hesitate to contact me.
